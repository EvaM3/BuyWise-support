Quick Fixes

Most issues are solved in under a minute.

Restore Purchases
  1. Open BuyWise.
  2. Go to the Paywall / Pro screen.
  3. Tap Restore Purchases.
  4. Make sure you’re signed into the same Apple ID used to purchase.

Tip: If you recently changed devices, a restore is often all you need.

App looks wrong / text is cut off

Check your iPhone’s Display Zoom and Text Size settings:

  • Settings → Display & Brightness → Display Zoom
  • Settings → Accessibility → Display & Text Size
I entered income/hours wrong — how do I edit?

Open BuyWise settings and update your income/hours anytime. Your calculations update instantly.
